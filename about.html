About

Two worlds,
one toolkit.

I'm a 4th-year CS student at UBC with a 4.30 GPA, building full-stack web apps, AI-powered tools, and interactive 3D experiences. My interest in CS started early — self-studying Python, taking game programming courses, and winning the Computer Science Award at graduation. That curiosity hasn't stopped since.

At UBC I've kept that same energy going. I built a MindMap feature from scratch at ZenNyx AI, handling the full pipeline from prompt engineering to a React-Konva rendering layer. More recently I shipped a production-ready booking system for Surrey Food Bank in a team of 5, working with a real client using React, Django, and PostgreSQL in an agile workflow. On the side I placed 2nd at the UBC Biztech Hellohacks hackathon and have been building personal projects — including an AI-powered chatbot with a locally run TTS model and layered prompt architecture.

Outside of the technical work, I produce music across cinematic, EDM, pop, and rock — always experimenting with new plugins, and taking courses in computer music and plugin programming. When I'm not in front of a screen I'm usually doing something social: ping pong, golf, pickleball, badminton, or finding any excuse for a bubble tea run or AYCE hotpot with friends.
